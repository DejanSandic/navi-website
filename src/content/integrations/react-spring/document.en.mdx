Navi works great with react-spring's [useTransition()](https://www.react-spring.io/docs/hooks/use-transition) hook. Together with the [`useViewElement()`](../../reference/react-components-hooks/#useviewelement) and [`useCurrentRoute()`](../../reference/react-components-hooks/#usecurrentroute) hooks, creating transitions between routes is a walk in the park.

```js
//---
//--- app.js <-- animated-app.js
//--- card.js <-- animated-card.js
//--- index.js <-- animated-index.js
//--- routes.js <-- animated-routes.js
```
